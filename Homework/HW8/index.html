A Bit of SCRABBLE with Drag-and-Drop
  • Player is given 8 tiles to form a word and the tiles are placed on the rack below.
  • On the board, the slots mark the DOUBLE WORD Squares and TRIPLE WORD Squares which are worth more points.
  • The letter is used to construct the words and its number indicates its point value.
  • Each word score will be added up to total score.
  • Note: Letters can only be drop next to the previous letter to make a new word!
